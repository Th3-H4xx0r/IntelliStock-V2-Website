Long Term

IntelliStock allows you to configure the bot to watch stocks for long term investments. Watch for the growth or decline of stocks on a monthly timeframe. This allows it to make decisions on when to buy and sell in the long run

Short Term

With short term watching, the bot will watch for stocks on a weekly basis and determine when to buy and sell a symbol

Day Trading

IntelliStock work well with day trading as it can make all of the trades within a day and everyday, this option works well with penny stocks that have lots of ups and downs.
